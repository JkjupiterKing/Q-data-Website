MRO

MRO stands for Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul, and it refers to the practices and activities that ensure equipment, machinery, and assets in any organization are properly maintained, repaired, and serviced. These activities are essential for maintaining the operational efficiency of businesses that rely on machinery, vehicles, or other assets to function.

Material Master

The Material Master is a central database in enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems, such as SAP or Oracle, that contains all the essential information about materials used in business processes. This data is crucial for various operations, including procurement, inventory management, production, sales, and distribution.
