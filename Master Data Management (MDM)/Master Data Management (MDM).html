Master Data Management (MDM)




E-commerce Master Data Management (MDM) focuses on managing and maintaining accurate, consistent, and centralized data related to an e-commerce business. This includes key data points such as products, customers, inventory, pricing, suppliers, and transactions. Effective MDM ensures that data across the e-commerce platform is reliable, up-to-date, and accessible to various systems like the website, ERP, CRM, and more. The goal is to create a single source of truth for all e-commerce-related data, which is crucial for delivering personalized customer experiences, optimizing inventory management, and ensuring smooth business operations.
